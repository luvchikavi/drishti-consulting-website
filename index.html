The Philosophy

Why Drishti?

In Sanskrit, Drishti (दृष्टि) means far more than simply "vision" or "sight." It represents the ability to see beyond the surface—to perceive with divine insight, focused intention, and inner wisdom. In yogic philosophy, drishti is the practice of concentrated gaze that leads to deeper understanding and clarity.

At Drishti Consulting, we embody this principle: helping businesses focus their vision on the right strategic point, cutting through complexity to see the path forward with crystal clarity. We transform abstract business goals into concrete, technology-driven solutions.
